PAN Card

PAN Card Consultant in Bangalore

The establishment of Taxcure Consultancy LLP took place in the year 2001, where it offers consultancy services concerning the procurement of PAN Cards in Bangalore. Permanent Account Number (PAN) is generally required in different areas like account opening with banks, enrollment for VAT or Service Tax, and receiving interest from fixed deposits. People above the age of 18 years; male or female are well qualified to apply for PAN card.

Evidence Required
  • ID Proof
  • Address Proof
  • Date of Birth Proof
  • 3 Photographs
Time consumed in process

Typically, it takes about 25 to 45 working days.

Methodology
  • Collection of compulsory documents from clients
  • Filling the application form
  • Submission of application in the back office
  • Delivery of PAN card to the client
  • In case you need help with your PAN card application, feel free to contact Taxcure Consultancy LLP at +91 95353 49494,or visit our office in Girinagar, Bangalore.
